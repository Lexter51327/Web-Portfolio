About Me

Hi, I'm Lexter Evangelista, a Web Designer and Developer who loves to turn ideas into interactive realities. I specialize in crafting beautiful, intuitive websites that are built on a foundation of strong, semantic code. What sets my work apart is my dual expertise. Not only do I focus on what users see (design) and interact with (front-end), but I also understand how it gets to them, thanks to my experience as a Network Technician. This unique perspective ensures that the websites I build are not just visually appealing but are also fast, secure, and stable. I believe in creating digital experiences that are a pleasure for users and a breeze for networks.
